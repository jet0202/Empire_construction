Empire Building & Construction was established in 2014 and operates from #17 Sheraton Park, Christ Church. The company has been the pioneering force behind the creation of dream spaces for individuals and corporate companies throughout the island. It has grown from strength to strength and has successfully completed more than (100) projects in the residential and commercial sector. Some of the services we offer include project management, architectural drawings, engineering, carpentry, masonry, steel bending, tiling, painting, plumbing, electrical installation, air conditioning and excavation.

Empire is equipped with the requisite expertise and wide-ranging experience to undertake construction projects with sourced responsibility. Projects are executed using state of the art designs, tools and project management techniques from concept to commissioning. We intend to be a one stop solution for all your construction needs. We deliver all projects in the committed time with the utmost care and consideration for quality. Every client is our asset and customer satisfaction is our main priority.

We envision a future where Empire Building & Construction is a household name and future, whereas our brand symbolizes superior community living of the highest standards of quality and customer delight.
